Our Solar System

Facts

Our Solar Systems consists of different kinds of celestial bodies that are bound to the Sun in the middle by gravity. There are 8 planets, their moons, asteroids and comets of different sizes.

You can learn more about the Solar System at NASA's official website.

Planets in order (from closest to the Sun)

  1. Mercury
  2. Venus
  3. Earth
  4. Mars
  5. Jupiter
  6. Saturn
  7. Uranus
  8. Neptune
